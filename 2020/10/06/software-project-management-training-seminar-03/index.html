Why we need document’s arrangements?

  • Project management’s gist
    • Know progress、analyze the issue，provide project predictability
  • Working communication’s proof
    • Oral statement are no guarantee write down in arrangement
  • Software test’s basis
    • Without document test workers can’t work
  • Train user’s reference
    • Help external people know the project’s inside story
  • Technical support’s helper
    • Help technical support’s helper be familiar with the system ,analyze the mistakes ,solve the problems, record the necessity of the history
    • The best value of the software is the reusability

The progress of making a whole set of document followed by the requirement is the progress of developing a software followed the standard.

The basic ideas of making documentsBasic principles of document management

  • Clarify the range of the documents to avoid blank and the fickleness
    • The big and whole documents are of no use
  • Stand in the readers’ shoes to avoid whistling jigs to a milestone
    • Keep the readers’ understanding as the standard to make the documents
  • Norm the nouns’ titles to keep the consistent
    • Keep the nouns’ titles consistent can cut down the ambiguity of the documents
  • Pay attention to the control of the versions to avoid attending to one and lose another
    • The documents in different periods record the whole progress of the development of the project
  • Use pictures, words, tables to guarantee the content is clear
    • Brief pictures and tables are better than many words

Basic principles of document management

  • Integrity
    • Project documents should cover the complete process of software development
  • Simplicity
    • Don’t waste too much time and energy writing documentation
  • Effectiveness
    • Documents must play a role in supporting decision and guiding reference
  • Continuity
    • Transformation of documents should have reasonable basis and continuously develop
